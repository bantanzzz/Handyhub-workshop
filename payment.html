Rental Policies

Rental Duration

Tools can be rented for a minimum of 1 day and up to 30 days. Longer rental periods may be available upon request.

Security Deposit

A security deposit may be required for certain tools. The deposit will be refunded upon return of the tool in good condition.

Late Returns

A late fee of 20% of the daily rate will be charged for each day the tool is returned after the agreed return date.

Damage Policy

Tools must be returned in the same condition as they were rented. Any damage beyond normal wear and tear will be charged accordingly.
